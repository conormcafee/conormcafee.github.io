About

I'm a designer & front-end developer from Ballymena, Northern Ireland with 3 years industry experience. Currently, I am working for dotRetailer in Belfast's Titanic Quarter.

My primary focus is on front-end development. To me, this is the most important part of the web development process as this is what your users will interact with the most.

I main aim is to build well executed websites, that not only look great on all devices - but also perform great. Living in Northern Ireland, we're not blessed with 4G connections in every town or even good broadband connections - so because of this I aim to optimise my websites as best I can, so that they load as quick as possible when the user is not on a great Internet connection.

Above you can see some of the companies that I have completed work for. Some of these projects were worked on in a freelance capacity, as a member of a team whilst employed by a company, or soley whilst employed by a company.
