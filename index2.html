About

I'm a designer & front-end developer from Ballymena, Northern Ireland with 3 years industry experience. Currently, I am working for dotRetailer in Belfast's Titanic Quarter.

I strive to build well executed websites, that not only look great on all devices - but also perform great. Living in Northern Ireland, we're not blessed with 4G connections in every town or even good broadband connections - because of this I aim to optimise my websites as best I can, so that they load as quick as possible when the user is not on a great Internet connection.
