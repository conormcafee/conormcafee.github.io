About

I'm a designer & front-end developer from Ballymena, Northern Ireland with 3 years industry experience. Currently, I am working for dotRetailer in Belfast's Titanic Quarter.

My aim is to build well executed websites, that look & perform great on all devices. We're now at a stage where a large chunk of are users browse from a mobile device, be it a mobile phone or tablet. Those users browsing over a 3G connection do not need to be downloading unnecessary content, I aim to prioritise whats necssary - giving users the best possible experience accessing the web.
