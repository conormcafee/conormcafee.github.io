About

I'm a designer & front-end developer from Ballymena, Northern Ireland with 3 years industry experience. Currently, I am working for dotRetailer in Belfast's Titanic Quarter.

My aim is to build well executed websites, that look & perform great on all devices. We're now at a stage where a large chunk of are users browse from a mobile device, albeit a mobile phone or tablet. Those users browsing over a 3G connection do not need to be downloading unnecessary content. My aim is to prioritise what content is necssary - giving users the best possible experience when accessing the web.

Networking

You can find me at local web events around Belfast such as Hustle & Refresh. These are always great for catching up with local designers & developers as well as meeting others. I have also attended Build on two occasions.

I really enjoy collaborating on projects with other designers and/or software developers, so if you need a front-end developer to help out on a subject feel free to give me a shout.
