Our Vision

Our vision is to deliver measurable step change in operational performance across Irish and UK Industry.

Vector follows a robust approach to identify the biggest performance issues and provide the coaching and tools to support the business to overcome them.

A Typical Engagement

01

Improve Operating Efficiency

We endeavour to deliver a 10-50% improvement in operating efficiency in any process through fixing problems that limit performance.

02

Due Diligence

Ahead of any project, we do a scoping exercise to identify if there is genuine opportunity in the business. If we find that there isn’t any opportunity, then we don’t progress. This stage is free of charge.

03

Assessment

If we do find opportunity, then we will progress to do an assessment to identify the specific opportunities and value them.

04

Project

Projects typically last 2-6 months and this is where we deliver the improvement. We work shoulder to shoulder with the team and leave them with the tools to drive future improvements themselves. There is no capex requirements.

05

Our Goal

Our end goal,is releasing a step change in performance and additional profit into your business.
